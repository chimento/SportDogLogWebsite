Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 22, 2025

SportDogLog respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when using the app.

Data Collection

  • No personal identifying information is collected.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized analytics to improve app performance and reliability.

Third-Party Services

SportDogLog may use Apple services (e.g. iCloud) but does not share data with external third parties.

Your Rights

You may delete your app data at any time from within the app. We do not store user accounts or personal identifiers.
