Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 22, 2025

Welcome to SportDogLog (“we,” “us,” “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the SportDogLog app or visit our website (SportDogLog.com).

1. Information We Collect

We prioritize minimal data collection:

  • Bird sightings, dog performance logs, and harvest events are stored locally or optionally synced via iCloud.
  • No personal identifying information is collected.
  • Optional, anonymized analytics may be used to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Data

  • Deliver core app features
  • Improve app reliability and usability
  • Debugging and performance monitoring

3. Data Storage & Security

All data resides locally or within the user’s iCloud account. No user account is required or stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Apple’s iCloud for syncing, and adhere to their privacy policies. We do not sell or share your data.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You can delete your data at any time within the app. No personal identifiers are stored by us.

6. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy. Continued use indicates acceptance of any updates.

7. Contact

Email us at support@sportdoglog.com
