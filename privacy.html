Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 22, 2025

Welcome to SportDogLog (“we,” “us,” “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the SportDogLog app or visit our website (SportDogLog.com) (collectively, “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

  • User Authentication Data: We use Clerk to manage user authentication. Clerk processes personal information (e.g., email, contact details) for sign-in only. We are the data controller; Clerk acts as a processor.
  • Logged Data: Your logs (dog performance, bird sightings, harvest events) are stored in Supabase with strict Row-Level Security, ensuring only you access your data.
  • Backups & Storage: Supabase automatically takes encrypted daily backups and supports point-in-time recovery (where enabled).
  • Optional Analytics: Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve app performance and reliability.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate users securely via Clerk.
  • Store and retrieve user-specific logs in Supabase.
  • Restore data when needed using encrypted backups.
  • Analyze anonymized usage for app enhancements.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Clerk: Authentication data is protected under Clerk’s SOC 2 security framework.
  • Supabase: Your logs are encrypted at rest with AES‑256, transmitted over TLS, and governed by robust RLS policies.
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups and optional point‑in‑time recovery enhance resilience.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Clerk: Handles user sign-in, social login, passkeys, and MFA. We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • Supabase: Manages data storage and backups—data remains under your control; RLS ensures access is restricted.

5. Your Rights

  • Manage or delete your data anytime within the app.
  • Only you (authenticated) can access your logs.
  • Questions or requests? Email support@sportdoglog.com.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy over time. Continued use signifies acceptance of any changes.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email support@sportdoglog.com.
